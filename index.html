Georgian Small Business Tax Calculator

A Google Sheets script that logs income, fetches NBG exchange rates, calculates 1% small business tax, and tracks your annual revenue — formatted for rs.ge.

1% Tax Auto-Calc NBG Exchange Rates GEL / USD / EUR / RUB rs.ge Compatible

How to Set Up

  1. Click the button below to open the Google Sheets template. Then go to File → Make a copy to save it to your own Drive.
  2. In your copy, go to Extensions → Apps Script. You will see Code.gs and Dialog.html already in place.
  3. Close the Apps Script tab and reload the spreadsheet. A new Tax Calculator menu will appear in the menu bar.
  4. Click Tax Calculator → Add Income. On first run, Google will ask you to authorize the script — follow the prompts and click Allow.
  5. Fill in the dialog (month, income date, currency, amount) and click Add Row. The script handles the rest.

Privacy & Terms

  • No financial data is logged or sent to any third-party service by the script.
  • The script operates entirely within your own Google Sheet — it reads and writes only to that spreadsheet.
  • The only external request the script makes is a query to the National Bank of Georgia public API to fetch exchange rates. No personal or financial data is included in that request.
  • All your data stays in your Google account. Nothing is collected, tracked, or shared by the script.
